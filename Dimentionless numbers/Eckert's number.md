>[! definition]
>$\large Ec=\frac{u^{2}}{c_{p}(T_{W}-T_{0}) }$

where:
- $u$ is the local flow velocity
- $c_{p}$ is the constant pressure specific heat
- $T_{W}$ is the wall temperature
- $T_{0}$ is the bulk fluid temperature
